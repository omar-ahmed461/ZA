Our Story

ZA3TAR

Za3tar is a modern youth clothing brand inspired by Middle Eastern identity, culture, and street style. It was created to redefine fashion by blending traditional influences with modern design, appealing to both local and global audiences.

The name Za3tar represents originality, cultural pride, and a strong connection to Middle Eastern roots. It reflects a bold and confident identity, encouraging young people to express themselves and embrace who they are.

The brand offers essential streetwear pieces including hoodies, T-shirts, denim, and accessories. Designs are clean, minimal, and modern, built around a strong black-and-white aesthetic.

More than just clothing, Za3tar is a movement. It blends cultural inspiration with global fashion trends, giving people the confidence to express their identity and personal style.
